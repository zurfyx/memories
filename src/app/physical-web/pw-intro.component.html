Physical Web

Sign in to start.

Signing in enables you to record with custom metadata, and keep track of your recorded URLs.

Memories supports Physical Web. By using Web Bluetooth and Eddystone Web Bluetooth, it is able to record online content to a beacon as a URL.

What is Physical Web?

Get beacons

On supported Android devices, you can use Beacon Toy to transform your phone into an Eddystone beacon. Otherwise, choose from a variety of beacon manufacturers.

Record a journey or story

Enter any journey or story, and click on the "Physical Web" menu option. Memories will help you record your favorite experiences onto your beacon.

Beacon will start broadcasting as soon as you finish the setup.

Complete

Place the beacon on an open space. Anyone who comes nearby* will see your broadcasted journey or story.

*In Android devices these notifications come by default on devices running 4.4 and above (when both bluetooth and location are turned on). Read more
